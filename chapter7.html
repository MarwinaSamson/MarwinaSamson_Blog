7:00 AM · Last Morning
Shopping album Baguio · Maharlika & SM

Gathering souvenirs and memories in the market.

OOne last breakfast at the Prince Plaza Hotel. The air is crisp, and the pine trees stand as silent witnesses to our final morning in the north. We have a few hours of freedom before the journey south begins.

We head to the Maharlika Shopping Center and SM Baguio. The mission is clear: "Pasalubong."

— 81 —
Pasalubong

Baskets are filled with strawberry jam, ube jam, and the woven textures of local crafts—pieces of Baguio to take home.

Maharlika is a maze of colors and scents. We haggle for knitted sweaters and find the best peanut brittle. It's the frantic, joyful ritual of travelers making sure no one at home is forgotten.

We return for a final lunch at the hotel. The mood is reflective; the cool air is already starting to feel like a memory.

Back to the Plains

Departure · Baguio → Manila

Check-out is official at 12:00 NN. The vans are loaded with luggage and boxes of pasalubong, smelling faintly of pine and strawberries. As we descend the zigzag roads, the temperature slowly begins to climb.

The long drive is filled with sleep and the occasional glimpse of the landscape shifting from mountain ridges to the flat, golden fields of the lowlands.
