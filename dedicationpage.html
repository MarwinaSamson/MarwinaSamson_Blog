Dedication — ✦ —

For

my batchmates, my professors,
and everyone who made these
seven days feel like a lifetime.

You laughed with me in lobbies
I did not know how to walk through.
You sat beside me in rooms
that smelled of ambition and coffee.
You whispered "this is so cool"
when I was too proud to say it first.

This is my attempt to hold on
to every moment we were present —
together, and wide awake.

Marwina 2025
