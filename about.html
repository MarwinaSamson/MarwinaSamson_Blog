About the Project

This is a record of seven days spent crossing Metro Manila with a group of IT students, walking into companies, asking questions, and trying to understand what the future looks like from the inside.

The Industry Visit and Educational Tour (IVET) is a required program for fourth-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology students at Western Mindanao State University. Each year, students travel to different cities to visit companies relevant to their field — observing real workflows, meeting professionals, and bridging the gap between academic learning and industry reality.

This storybook is my personal documentation of that week. It is not a formal report. It is a collection of notes, photographs, observations, and honest impressions gathered across each day from the chaos of the airport at 3 AM to the quiet of the dormitory at night, and every company visit in between.

The goal was simple: to remember it properly.
