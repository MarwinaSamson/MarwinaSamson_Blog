Chapter V · Day 4 V

The Ascent: Bound for Tagaytay

6:30 AM · DJM Dormitory

The morning begins with the quiet ritual of breakfast at the dorm at 6:30 AM. There is a different weight to the air today—a shift from office towers to the open ridge.

We leave the city behind at 8:00 AM. The bus hums as we climb the winding roads of Cavite. Through the window, the landscape stretches out, turning from grey concrete to deep, vibrant greens.

It’s a long drive, but the shared silence and the changing horizon make the distance feel like a necessary bridge between two worlds.

By the time the temperature drops, we know we're close. The bus slows as we approach the summit, the air now smelling of pine and damp earth.

We arrive at our first destination by 9:30 AM, stepping out into a breeze that feels like a clean break from the week's intensity.

The Summit V

People's Park in the Sky

Mid-Morning · High Altitude

Standing at the edge of People's Park, the world feels expansive. The mist clings to the unfinished scaffolding of the old palace, creating an atmosphere that is both haunting and beautiful.

We wander through the view decks, looking down at the lake. From this height, everything below seems manageable, like a miniature map of our own lives. The wind is sharp, but we don't mind.

We capture the textures of the stone and the vastness of the horizon. It’s a moment of collective pause, a chance to simply look out without a schedule to meet.

The Fairground V

Sky Ranch: Optional Tour

Afternoon · Carnival Lights

The Sky Eye dominates the skyline as we enter Sky Ranch. It’s a shift from the quiet reflection of the park to the vibrant noise of the fairground.

The air is filled with the scent of street food and the sound of mechanical joy. Some of us brave the rides while others prefer the slower pace of the walkways, watching the Ferris wheel turn slowly against the clouds.

— 5 —
Leisure

We break for a packed lunch at 12:00 NN, sitting together and sharing what we’ve seen so far. There’s a lightness here that we’ve all needed.

By 2:30 PM, it's time to head back. We leave the neon and the music behind, the bus now full of people ready for the long stretch home.

Nightfall · Return V

Back to the Base

6:30 PM · DJM Dormitory

The city welcomes us back with its familiar hum and glow. By 6:30 PM, we are back at the dorm for dinner, the ridge now a memory of cold wind and high views.

We eat in the shared space, the day’s fatigue setting in but the energy still high. It’s our last major group outing, and the weight of that transition is starting to feel real.

— 7 —
Final Thoughts

We plan for tomorrow, but for tonight, we just rest. The dorm feels different—full of the people we've become over this journey.

The lights go out, and we fall asleep to the sound of the city we've finally learned to navigate.
