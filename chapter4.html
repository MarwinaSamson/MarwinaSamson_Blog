Control & Order: MMDA

9:00 AM · MMDA Command Center

The pulse of the city is visible here. Wall-to-wall screens flicker with the life of Manila’s arteries, captured in real-time.

We arrive at the MMDA Command Center after leaving at 7:30 AM. It feels like stepping into the brain of the metropolis. Precision, monitoring, and the constant hum of data being processed to keep millions moving.

It’s a stark reminder of how Information Technology isn't just about code—it's about the infrastructure that supports human movement and safety across a vast urban landscape.
